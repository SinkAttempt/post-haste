### Key Insight
Zero quality arcade idle games exist in the mail/sorting space on mobile. Steam has proven the theme works (9.2 rating on Catto's). The gap is translation to one-handed portrait play.

### Control Scheme Benchmarks
- **Universal pattern:** Virtual joystick + auto-proximity + visual stack
- **Sorting innovation needed:** No arcade idle has sorting decisions (all auto-route items)
- **Hybrid approach:** Joystick movement between stations + swipe-to-sort at the sorting desk
- **Proven swipe-sort:** Reigns (binary swipe), Water Sort (tap-to-pour), Grocery Sort (flick-to-bin)
